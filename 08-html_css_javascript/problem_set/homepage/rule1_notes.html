Rule #1: Don't Lose Money
    The Rule #1 Website has tools to calculate "The Big 5": important values for finding the intrinsic value of a company.
    Morningstar has the historical data required to plug into the equations.
    Each of these numbers should be growing at >10% on average over the last 10 years, and growth should not be slowing down.

    Calculating the Return on Invested Capital requires: NOPAT (Net Operating Profit After Taxes), Equity, Debt, OR just look it up on Morningstar.

    Sales (Revenue) Growth Rate, calculation requires: Current Sales, Initial Sales, and Time Period (current year - initial year) OR Revenue over 10 years, calculate growth rate each year.

    EPS Growth Rate (Earnings Per Share) requires: Current EPS, Initial EPS, Time Period (current year - initial year), OR EPS over 10 years, calculate growth rate each year.

    Operating Cash Flow Growth Rate requires: OLDEST Cash Flow from Operating Activities, NEWEST Cash Flow from Operating Activities, Years between the two OR Operating Cash Flow over 10 years, calculate growth rate each year.

    Equity (Book Value Per Share) Growth Rate requires: Current Equity, Initial Equity, Time Period (current year - initial year), OR Equity over 10 years, calculate growth rate each year.

    Calculating Sticker Price

    1. Current EPS (TTM EPS).
    2. Future EPS growth rate (estimated).
      • Use the historical Equity (BVPS) growth rate, then the EPS, Sales, & Cash Flow growth rates to establish an estimate.
      • Compare to analyst's 5 year projection found at zacks.com in "detailed estimates".
      • Use the lower of these 2 values.
    3. Future PE (estimated):
      • Default future PE = EPS growth rate estimate x 2.
      • Compare Default future PE to historical average PE, use lower of the 2.
    4. Minimum Acceptable Rate of Return % (Rule #1 MARR = 15%)

    Other Factors

    1. Long term debt should be less than 3x annual free cash flow.
    2. Check out the Financial Visualizations on finviz.com to screen stocks based on “big 5” as available
